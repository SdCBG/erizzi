Professor of Mechanics of Solids and Structures


Research keywords
- Material modelling: quasi-brittle materials, geomaterials, composite materials, metals; elastoplasticity, elastic degradation, damage, anisotropy, fluid-saturated porous media;
- Material and structural failure: failure mechanics, material instabilities, strain localization, Portevin-Le Chatelier (PLC) effect, regularization of finite element computations;
- Structural themes: structural analysis of historic construction, modeling of Paderno d'Adda (1889) and Brivio (1917) bridges, statics of masonry arches by Limit Analysis and Discrete Element Method (DEM), tuning of Tuned Mass Dampers, modeling of High Strain Rate effects, Moving Load dynamical problems, parameter identification and model updating by inverse analysis.
